Common questions about miniMerch
Minima is a Layer-1 blockchain that runs completely on-device. Unlike other blockchains that require powerful servers, Minima nodes can run on phones, IoT devices, or desktop computers. This makes it perfect for truly decentralized applications like miniMerch.
For selling with miniMerch: Yes, your node needs to be online to receive orders. However, Minima's mobile app runs efficiently in the background with minimal battery drain. For buying: No, you just need a node running when you place an order.
Anything legal! miniMerch supports physical goods (with postal delivery), digital products (with email/URL delivery), and services. You set the shipping options, prices, and product details.
Customers can pay with MxUSD (a Minima-native stablecoin) or Minima. Payments go directly to your address — no intermediary, no payment processor, no escrow.
Yes! Unlike Shopify or other platforms where your store can be deleted by the company, your miniMerch shop is tied to your Minima node. As long as you run a node, your shop exists. There's no company that can shut you down.
Buyers see a clean shop MiniDapp with your product images, descriptions, and prices. Multi-product shops show all items in a grid. Buyers can add multiple items to a cart and pay in one transaction. Delivery addresses are encrypted — only you can read them.
Download it from GitHub. It's available for macOS and Windows. The Studio app is a visual shop builder — no coding or terminal needed. Fill in your details, add products, click Generate, and your shop installs directly to your node.
