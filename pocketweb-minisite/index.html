Anyone can browse

1

Build your site

Create HTML files or use PocketWeb's built-in Site Generator — no coding needed. Zip them up (max 50 KB).

2

Publish with PocketFS

Upload your ZIP to PocketFS. It gets a permanent Mx address on the Minima blockchain. Only you can update it.

3

Share & browse with PocketWeb

Anyone running PocketWeb can search for and browse your site. Sites spread peer-to-peer through the Minima network.
